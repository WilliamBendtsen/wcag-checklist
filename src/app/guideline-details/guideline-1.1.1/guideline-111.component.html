1.1.1 - Non-text content

This success criterion aims to make non-text content accessible through the use of a text alternative

Who does this benefit?

  • People who have difficulty perceiving visual content
  • People who are deaf, hard of hearing or who are having trouble understanding audio information for any reason
  • People who have difficulty understanding the meaning of photographs, drawings and other images

How to meet this criterion

  • Make sure all images have alt-text
  • Make sure all objects have aria-labels
More details on how to meet this criterion

Examples

The image indicating wether the print job has color or not

Code example:
<img src="img/bw-icon.png" alt="Print job is Black & White">

Icons indicating actions or status

Code examples:

<button aria-label="Edit staff member location access" none;"></button>

<button aria-label="Delete user"></button>

<div aria-label="Status icon"></div>

<span aria-label="Checkmark icon"></span>
