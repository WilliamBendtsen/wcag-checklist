2.4.3 - Focus Order

This success criterion aims to make it so keyboard users navigate in a correct order

Who does this benefit?

  • People with mobility impairments who must rely on keyboard access for operating a page
  • People with disabilities that make reading difficult can become disoriented when tabbing takes focus someplace unexpected
  • People with visual impairments can become disoriented when tabbing takes focus someplace unexpected or when they cannot easily find the content surrounding an interactive element.
  • People who use a screen magnifier at a high level of magnification. Such a user may interpret a field in the wrong context if the focus order is not logical.

How to meet this criterion

  • Placing the interactive elements in an order that follows sequences and relationships within the content
