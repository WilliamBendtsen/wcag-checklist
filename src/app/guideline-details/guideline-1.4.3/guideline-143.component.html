1.4.3 - Contrast (Minimum) Official 1.4.3 WCAG Docs

This success criterion aims to make it so the text in question can be seen by more people

Who does this benefit?

  • People with color vision deficiency
  • People with generally low vision

How to meet this criterion

(For text that is less than 18 point if not bold and less than 14 point if bold)
  • Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 exists between text and background behind the text
(For text that is at least 18 point if not bold and at least 14 point if bold)
  • Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 exists between text and background behind the text

Note:
This browser extension can automatically detect if the contrast ratio of an entire site meets this criterion

#E6E6E6 foreground against #435063 background
